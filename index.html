About

AMI-HACKS

Usually AMI-HACKS is a 24-hour hackathon, or “creative marathon”. We were founded in 2019 and are now in our 3rd iteration, having grown from 122 to 700+ attendees. We provide a unique space for hundreds of passionate technologists to share their experiences, meet new people and learn something new - gaining valuable skills in the process.

About

AMI-HACKS 2021

In light of the COVID-19 situation, we’ve taken the decision to move this year’s event online. Though we’re sad to lose our delightful in-person environment for this year, we’re taking this opportunity to innovate, and engage thousands of attendees digitally. We’re determined to build an awesome space for everyone to keep being creative, and work on unique projects together
